ECONOMIC IMPACT STATEMENT: MINNESOTA DIGITAL BRIDGE
PROJECT: Arcana-Credit (ARC/CC) Liquidity Engine
TRUSTEE: Damarieus Jones

The Damarieus Jones Community Innovation Group is establishing a high-value digital liquidity bridge in Minnesota. By transitioning $89,134,938,400 in verified digital settlements (ARC/CC) into an OCC 1186-compliant framework, we enable local businesses to utilize these assets as secondary collateral. This grant ($2,000) provides the initial legal formation (LLC) and technical gas required to activate this multi-billion dollar estate for the benefit of the Minnesota state economy.
