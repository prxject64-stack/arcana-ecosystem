# Arcana Constitution (v2.0)

**The Founding Document of the Sovereign Arcana Ecosystem**

**Preamble**  
We, the Citizens of Arcana, in order to form a more perfect sovereign economy, establish justice, ensure domestic tranquility, provide for the common defense, promote the general welfare, and secure the blessings of liberty to ourselves and our posterity, do ordain and establish this Constitution for the Arcana State-within-a-State.

---

## Article I: Monetary Sovereignty
The Treasury holds 136,000,000,000 Credit Coin Private (CC) in perpetuity.  
No inflation. No additional minting. Releases governed solely by verifiable market demand via on-chain oracle.

---

## Article II: Sovereign Sanctuary
The Arcana Node is a private sanctuary.  
No data logging. No KYC. No third-party access. Citizens' transactions are inviolable.

---

## Article III: Supreme Shield
The Sovereign Shield is the supreme law.  
- 0.5% transaction cap on all non-governance addresses.  
- 60-second cooldown between transactions.  
No exceptions. No overrides. Bots, whales, and rugs are mathematically impossible.

---

## Article IV: Citizenship
Citizenship is earned through sacrifice.  
- Burn 100 CC to the dead address to naturalize.  
- Maintain through contribution (revenue share eligibility).  
Soulbound Passport NFT grants fast-lane node access and quarterly revenue share.

---

## Article V: Revenue & Wealth
30% of all system revenue (Bridge tolls, node access fees, future services) flows to the Citizen Treasury.  
Distributed quarterly on-chain to verified citizens.

---

## Article VI: Governance
Citizens vote on amendments via weighted token holdings.  
Founder retains veto for first 24 months to protect the vision.

---

## Article VII: Expulsion
Malicious actors (proven via on-chain evidence) may be stripped of citizenship by 66% citizen vote.
